üîÑ Revoking Access

You can revoke our app's access to your Meta data at any time by:

  1. Going to your Meta Settings
  2. Navigating to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Finding "Meta Ads Tracker" and clicking "Remove"

Once revoked, we will no longer be able to access your data, and any stored tokens will become invalid.
